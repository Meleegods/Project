The Trans Advocacy is an organization that strives to promote and protect the rights of transgender individuals. Our team provides support, resources, and education to individuals who are barred from services or experiencing discrimination. By providing them with the information they need to have awareness of their rights, we allow them to make informed descisions about their lives. By joining our community, you will gain access to a warm, welcoming community that will act as a pillar of support throughout your transition, as well as a vast array of tools that will help you navigate the challenges of being a transgender individual in today's society. All people have the right to live a life free from discrimination and oppression, and we are here as your lifewatch through life's trials and tribulations.
